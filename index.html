About

About Me

My name is Kathryn Byrnes, and I'm a senior at the University of Michigan. I'm majoring in Cognitive Science with minors in Computer Science and UX Design.

I'm from District One in Virginia, and local politics have always played a big role in my and my family's lives. I'm passionate about voter education and accessible voter resources. My hope is that clearly organized, easily accessible information could help citizens become more knowledgable and involved in local politics and the causes they care about.

Local elections are often overlooked, but they are incredibly important--especially primaries that determine which major party candidates will appear on the ballot

I chose to focus on the House of Representatives candidates for my district because I noticed that there is a surplus of candidates (particularly from the Democratic party), which can be it harder to keep track of and learn about all options.
